Sisterhood

The Sisterhood of the Woodbury Jewish Center is comprised of a group of energetic, motivated and dedicated women who work together to plan events and programs that are inspirational and exciting. Our focus is on outreach and inclusion, within the congregation, local communities and the greater Jewish world.
Along with programs such as book clubs and Lunch and Learns, the Sisterhood participates in clothing, food, toy, prom dress, and blood drives for those in need, as well as UJA Federation events. We sponsor kiddushes for the congregation, seders for the Religious School, holiday care packages for college students, and acknowledge those who have suffered a loss in our shul.
